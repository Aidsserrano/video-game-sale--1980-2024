# 🎮 Video Game Market Analysis (1980 - 2024)

## 📊 **Project Overview**
---
This project performs an **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** on a comprehensive dataset of video game sales. The goal is to identify which **genres, platforms, and publishers** have dominated the market over the last four decades and to prepare the data for future predictive modeling.

## 🔍 **Key Insights & Interpretations**
---

### 1. **Total Sales by Genre**
> **Insight:** This chart identifies the **"powerhouse"** genres of the industry. Typically, **Action and Sports** lead in total volume, suggesting that broad-market appeal is the most reliable driver of high revenue.

### 2. **Evolution of Video Game Sales (1980 - 2024)**
> **Insight:** This time-series plot tracks the industry's growth. You can clearly see the **"Golden Age"** of physical media in the mid-2000s, followed by shifts that correlate with the rise of **digital storefronts** and **mobile gaming**.

### 3. **Annual Total Sales by Console**
> **Insight:** This visualization highlights the **hardware life cycle**. It shows how consoles like the **PS2, Wii, and Xbox 360** rose to dominance and eventually faded as next-generation hardware took over.

### 4. **Publisher Market Share & Genre Specialization**
> **Insight:** Using this treemap, we can see which publishers **"own"** specific genres. It reveals whether the market is diversified or if a few **"AAA" giants** (like Nintendo or EA) control the majority of the high-value intellectual properties.

### 5. **Game Count Heatmap: Genre vs. Console**
> **Insight:** This heatmap reveals the **"Software DNA"** of each platform. For example, it shows if a specific console was a **"RPG powerhouse"** or if another was the go-to platform for **"Racing"** and **"Shooters."**
